EduTrackr

EduTrackr is a student grade-tracking system designed to help students and faculty efficiently manage academic records. It provides an intuitive and user-friendly platform for monitoring grades, tracking progress, and ensuring academic transparency. Developed as part of our Intermediate Programming course, this project aims to streamline academic record management while fostering collaboration and innovation.
